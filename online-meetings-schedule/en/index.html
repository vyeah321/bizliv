Online Meeting Schedule
Supporting Efficient Meeting Management

Efficiently manage your time with just a copy-paste of the invitation. An essential app for business professionals.

Download Now

Key Features

Automatic Schedule Addition

Automatically analyzes and adds topics, times, and URLs from Zoom invitations to your schedule. Also compatible with other platforms like Google Meet and Skype.

Smart URL Extraction

When you paste text containing URLs, it automatically extracts only the URLs. Easily manage multiple meeting links.

Convenient Notification Feature

Notifies you of the start and end times of meetings. You can also tap the start time notification screen to join the meeting directly.

Calendar Integration

Automatically adds schedules to your default calendar or Google Calendar. Manage all your schedules in one place.

Easy-to-Understand Meeting List

Meetings are listed in chronological order, making it easy to see your next meeting at a glance. Achieve efficient schedule management.

How to Use

1. Add Schedule by Simply Pasting the Invitation

Just paste invitation text like the following into the app's text box, and the schedule will be automatically added: 

Topic: Online Meeting
Time: May 25, 2020 09:00 PM Osaka, Sapporo, Tokyo
Join Zoom Meeting
https://us02web.zoom.us/j/12345678900?pwd=ABCDEFGHIJKLMNOPQRSTU

2. Join Meetings with One Tap

Join meetings immediately by simply tapping the "Join" button on the meeting information screen or the start time notification screen. No need to search for URLs, allowing for smooth meeting participation.
