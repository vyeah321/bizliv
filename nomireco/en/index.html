Key Features

Easy Management of Drinking and Alcohol-Free Days with Calendar

Record your drinking and alcohol-free days on the calendar for easy viewing. This helps you understand your drinking patterns and supports a healthier lifestyle.

  • Easily record drinking and alcohol-free days
  • Manage drinking events and evening drinks with event registration

Visualize Your Health Status with Graphs

Check past drinking days and weight changes in graph form, and track progress towards your target weight and drinking frequency.

  • Graph your weight and number of drinking days
  • Monitor progress towards your health goals

Record heart rate and walking data by integrating with Apple Watch, and check the effects of drinking on your body.

  • Track heart rate and walking data
  • Comprehensively manage exercise volume and health risks
