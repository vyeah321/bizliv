Privacy Policy

1. Collection and Use of Personal Information

The app "NomiReco" may collect personal information from users, such as name and email address. This information will only be used for specific purposes such as providing customer support and handling inquiries. It will not be shared or disclosed to third parties without the user's consent.

2. Data Storage and Security

Data related to drinking events and health information recorded by the app will be securely stored on the user's device or iCloud when signed in with an Apple ID. All data is encrypted to prevent unauthorized access by third parties.

3. Access Permissions

This app may request access to your calendar, contacts, location, and other permissions. These permissions are used only for the features of the app (such as managing drinking events and schedule management) and will not be used for other purposes.

4. Usage Data

This app collects usage data (in a non-personally identifiable format) using Google Analytics and Firebase to improve services and enhance features. For more details, please refer to the privacy policies of Google and Firebase.

5. Advertising

This app uses Google AdMob for ad delivery. Cookies and tracking technologies may be used during ad display. For more information, please review Google's advertising policies.

6. Changes to the Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as needed. Any changes will be notified on this page.
