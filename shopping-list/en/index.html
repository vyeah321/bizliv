Shopping List
Support for Efficient Shopping

Save time and effort with automatic genre-based sorting and family sharing features. Your ally for busy modern life.

Download Now

Main Features

Automatic Genre-based Sorting

Organizes items clearly by automatically identifying genres from product names. Optimized for efficient navigation through supermarket sections.

Reminder-style Checklist

Easy shopping checks with a familiar reminder format. Saves time by avoiding repeated visits to the same section, allowing you to complete shopping via the shortest route.

Automatic List Formatting

Converts product lists sent by family or friends into usable checklists with just a copy and paste. Automatically formats bulk-pasted product lists for easy use.

Useful in These Scenarios

Ally for Busy Families with Children

For parents juggling childcare and household chores, weekend shopping can be a challenging task. Especially when family gatherings require more shopping than usual. With this app, you can quickly add requests from family members and shop efficiently in stores using genre-sorted lists.

Strong Support for Those Living Alone

For college students who have just started living alone, cooking at home is appealing but shopping can be confusing. By asking parents for recommended ingredients and importing the list into the app, necessary items are organized by genre, allowing for smooth shopping without getting lost.

Time-Saving for Working Couples

For working couples, both busy with jobs, finding time for shopping can be challenging. Using this app's sharing feature, you can quickly import shopping lists sent by your partner via messaging app. Thanks to the automatically organized, easy-to-read lists, you can shop efficiently between work tasks and meet family requests.
