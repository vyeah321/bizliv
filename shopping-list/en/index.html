Main Features

Automatic Genre-based Sorting

Organizes items clearly by automatically identifying genres from product names. Optimized for efficient navigation through supermarket sections.

Reminder-style Checklist

Easy shopping checks with a familiar reminder format. Saves time by avoiding repeated visits to the same section, allowing you to complete shopping via the shortest route.

Automatic List Formatting

Converts product lists sent by family or friends into usable checklists with just a copy and paste. Automatically formats bulk-pasted product lists for easy use.

Rakuten Market Integration

Search for entered items on Rakuten Market and purchase them online via product page links. All Rakuten Market products are available for purchase.

Ally for Busy Families with Children

For parents juggling childcare and household chores, weekend shopping can be a challenging task. With this app, you can quickly add requests from family members and shop efficiently using genre-sorted lists.

Using the sharing feature, you can quickly import shopping lists sent by your partner. Thanks to the automatically organized lists, you can shop efficiently between work tasks and meet family requests.
